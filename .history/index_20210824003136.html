Discover the city of Leipzig kayaking with us

Discover the "water city of Leipzig". No matter whether by paddle or canoe or rowing boat.The city and its surroundings are among the most beautiful paddling areas in Saxony. Leipzig offers an incomparable combination of urban flair, unique Wilhelminian style architecture Unesco World Heritage Site and natural paradise Leipzig floodplain forest. Overall, the city has almost 300 km of extensive river and canal landscape.
